WHAT WE DO

We offer services that cover the entire design and build of a project including architecture, interior design, engineering, construction, fit-out, and project management. Clients can pick from our individual services or opt for a complete package covering a project from start to finish.

Furniture:


Our experienced architecture team combines creativity and practical knowledge to design iconic buildings that are environmentally and socially sustainable. Working closely to understand the individual needs of clients, we develop comprehensive plans to meet your budget and brief.

Interior Designing:


Our professional interior designers carefully study rooms and project goals to strategically plan the space to be both aesthetically pleasing and functional. 3D imaging lets us show our clients what the finished space will be like, to ensure our clients are happy.

Engineering:


Our in-house Structural, Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) Engineers, are experts in construction codes and building proportions to ensure practical aspects of a building work effectively.

Construction:


Our skilled in-house construction team brings our design ideas to reality. Each is an expert in their craft, including masons, painters, polishers, plumbers, and carpenters. And our site supervisors are experienced in running the team effectively to ensure safety and quality standards are always upheld and deadlines are met.

Project Management:


Landscape PLC vouches to take over your project’s management under its control. You no longer have to spend your time and energy keeping the details in check. With our fortnightly and monthly reports, and daily site monitoring we make sure you are aware of the timelines and progress of your anticipated civil construction and interior fit-outs.

Materials:


We source the latest building materials that provide the best solutions to enhance and help future-proof your project.

Furniture:


Bespoke furniture maximises the look and function of a space. We have our own team of artisan makers, skilled in producing individual pieces that compliment the design theme. From minimalistic, contemporary pieces to traditional furniture, we create pieces for homes, offices, shops and communal outside areas.

Discover how our integrated approach and personalised service is creating remarkable spaces. Take a look at our Design Gallery or get in touch at azinnovation.com.
